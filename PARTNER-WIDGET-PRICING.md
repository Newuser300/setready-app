# SetReady — Training & Certificates Widget

### Partner licensing & pricing (embeddable widget)

**What it is:** an embeddable widget that drops SetReady's **Training Modules** and **Certificates of Completion** into a partner's portal (e.g., 1ov1 / ShowCatcher). The widget runs on SetReady's servers; the partner pastes one snippet. Branded "Powered by SetReady."

**Scope:** Training Modules + Certificates **only** — no roster, casting, messaging, or data access.

*All prices in CAD. 12-month term. Setup fee applies to both options.*

## Option A — Talent access the modules **FREE** (partner-funded / promo code)

The partner's talent redeem a **promo code** and complete the modules and certificate at **no cost to them**. The partner funds access through a monthly license. Best for adoption — every rostered performer can get trained and certified.

**Monthly license — tiered by *active* talent** (a performer who opens a module or holds a live certificate that month; inactive roster is free):

| Tier | Active talent / month | Monthly license |
|---|---|---|
| Launch (pilot) | up to 25 | **$99** |
| Starter | up to 75 | **$199** |
| Growth | up to 200 | **$449** |
| Scale | up to 500 | **$899** |
| Enterprise | 500+ | Custom |

- **Certificates included** at no extra charge.
- **Annual prepay:** pay 10 months, get 12 (~17% off).
- **Promo code** is scoped to the partner and revocable — SetReady controls how many free redemptions it covers per month (matched to the tier).

**Partner's view:** predictable flat monthly cost, talent get a free perk, strong take-up. SetReady earns recurring license revenue.

---

## Option B — Talent **pay $9.99** for the modules

Each talent pays the standard **$9.99** (one-time) to complete the modules and receive their certificate. The partner pays **no monthly license** — only the setup fee — and earns a referral cut on every paying talent.

| Who pays | Amount | Split |
|---|---|---|
| Talent (one-time) | **$9.99** | — |
| → SetReady keeps | **$6.99** | 70% |
| → Partner referral | **$3.00** | 30% |

- **No monthly license** — revenue is purely per paying talent.
- **Certificates included** in the $9.99.
- Optional **annual re-certification** at $9.99 (same split) if the partner wants certs kept current.
- SetReady collects payment (Stripe) and pays the partner's referral share monthly; nothing to reconcile on his end.

**Partner's view:** near-zero cost to switch on ($500 setup only), makes ~$3/certified performer, offers a real credential his competitors don't have. Depends on talent willingness to pay.
